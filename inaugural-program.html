Inaugural Program

The inaugural function of IEEE TENCON 2019 will be held on Friday, 18 th October, 2019 from 10.00 AM to 11.00 PM at. Vembanad, Hotel Grand Hyatt, Bolgatty, Kochi. Shri. Arif Mohammad Khan, Hon. Governor of Kerala has consented to be the Chief Guest and inaugurate the conference.
We invite all the delegates to the inaugural function. Delegates are requested to be seated inside the hall before 9.45 AM. The inaugural program schedule is given below:


Schedule

Time Agenda
10:00 AM National Anthem
10:05 AM Welcome Address, Dr. K.R. Suresh Nair, General Chair, TENCON 2019
10:10 AM Special Address, Mr. Stephen Welby, Executive Director and COO, IEEE
10:20 AM Presidential Address, Dr. Akinori Nishihara, Director, IEEE Region 10
10:30 AM Inauguration by lighting the lamp
10:35 AM Inaugural Address
- Shri. Arif Mohammad Khan
- Hon. Governor of Kerala
10:55 AM Vote of Thanks, Dr. Sameer S.M, Chair, IEEE Kerala Section
11:00 AM National Anthem
