Conference Venue


Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatti, Kerala

Hyatt Hotels Corporation is an American multinational hospitality company that manages and franchises luxury hotels, resorts, and vacation properties. The Hyatt Corporation came into being upon purchase of the Hyatt House, at Los Angeles International Airport, on September 27, 1957. Perched on 26 acres of plush green land on the serene Bolgatty Island, Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatti is a waterfront urban resort overlooking the backwaters of Vembanad Lake.
