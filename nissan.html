Cyber security -- panel discussion




New Age Cyber Attacks—Strategies to Mitigate them and Remediate
Mahindra Electric

Head – Advance Technology, Mahindra Electric Mobility Limited

The cyber threat landscape has grown by leaps and bounds in recent years. … So much, that even the World Economic Forum’s report of 2018 recognizes the breakdown of information infrastructure and networks due to large-scale cyber-attacks, as one of the top five global risks that impact people, in terms of their “likelihood.”
The silver lining though is that the strategies that can be employed to mitigate such risks and maintain business continuity, are evolving as well. Do join us for an engaging panel discussion to know more about such strategies from some of the leading cyber security specialists in the country
When: 4 PM – 5 PM, October 19, 2019
Where: The Grand Hyatt, Kochi.
Moderator: Tarun Kumar, Chief Information Security Officer, Nissan Digital India.
Contact number: 9810307790
Tarun.kumar@nissanmotor.com

Tarun brings two decades of experience in the areas of cyber security/information security/IT risk management. As the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) for Nissan Digital Hub, he is responsible for overseeing the management of cyber security function at Nissan, besides providing leadership to the global teams and capability creation projects.
