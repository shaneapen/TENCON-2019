Special Industrial Track (SIT)


IEEE TENCON 2019 provides an extra platform for students, young professionals, and experts from academia and Industries as well as start-ups to interact brainstorm to leverage mutual benefits. This one day event being held on 19th October is open to all registered attendees of TENCON 2019 free of cost. It is also possible to separately register and attend this track. Industries and Start-ups interested to participate are requested to contact any of the organizers given below:

Organisers:


  • IEEE Region 10
  • IEEE Kerala Section

  • Chris GwoGiun Lee, National Cheng Kung University, Taiwan (clee@mail.ncku.edu.tw)
  • Takako Hashimoto, Chiba University of Commerce, Japan (takako@cuc.ac.jp)
  • Amit Kumar, BioAxis DNA Research Centre (P) Ltd, India (amitkr@ieee.org)
